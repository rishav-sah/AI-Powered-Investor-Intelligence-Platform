# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name

AI-Powered Investor Intelligence Platform

## 1. Business Overview

The AI-Powered Investor Intelligence Platform is designed to help investors and analysts quickly understand the financial health and performance of a company by analyzing annual reports and financial statements.

The platform transforms lengthy financial documents into easily consumable business insights, dashboards, and conversational responses, enabling faster and more informed decision-making.

## 2. Business Problem

Financial reports often contain hundreds of pages of information, making it difficult and time-consuming for investors and analysts to identify critical insights.

Users currently spend significant effort manually reviewing reports to understand:

- Revenue performance
- Profitability
- Financial position
- Business risks
- Growth opportunities

A faster and more efficient approach is required to extract and present these insights.

## 3. Business Objectives

The platform should:

- Simplify the analysis of annual reports
- Reduce manual effort required to review financial documents
- Automatically identify key financial metrics
- Highlight business risks and growth opportunities
- Present information through an intuitive dashboard
- Enable users to ask follow-up questions and receive contextual responses

## 4. Functional Requirements

### Document Upload

Users should be able to upload annual reports and financial statements.

### Automated Insight Generation

The system should automatically extract key financial information from uploaded reports.

### Financial KPI Extraction

The platform should identify and present:

- Revenue
- Net Income
- Operating Income
- Cash Flow
- Total Assets
- Total Liabilities

### Business Insight Generation

The platform should provide:

- Key Risk Factors
- Growth Drivers
- Executive-level financial summaries

### Interactive AI Assistant

Users should be able to ask natural language questions about uploaded financial reports.

Example questions:

- Why did revenue increase in 2024?
- What are the major risks highlighted in the report?
- What are the key growth opportunities for the company?
- Compare the financial performance with previous years.

The assistant should provide contextual responses based on the uploaded documents.

### Dashboard

Users should be able to view financial metrics and business insights through a centralized dashboard.

## 5. Expected Business Value

The platform enables investors and analysts to reduce the time spent reviewing financial reports while improving access to critical business insights.

By automating document analysis, providing conversational access to information, and presenting insights in a structured dashboard, the solution supports faster decision-making, improved productivity, and a better overall user experience.
